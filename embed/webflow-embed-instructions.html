üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Chatbot not appearing: Check that you've replaced the Railway domain and published your site
  • CORS errors: Ensure your domain is added to the ALLOWED_ORIGINS environment variable
  • Styling conflicts: The widget uses isolated CSS, but check for z-index conflicts
  • Mobile responsiveness: The widget automatically adapts to mobile screens
